About School Compare

School Compare allows you to compare multiple NSW Australia Public and Private schools so you can best determine the right fit for your child or loved one.

School Compare utilises NSW Department of Education datasets in order to have the most accurate data involved in comparing NSW Schools.
This allows us to display unbiased data as we do not ask individual schools to supply us with any information/details.

To further display our dedication to unbiased reporting of information, you may view the School Compare Github Page. Here you may find all the code used to develop, run and maintain this site.
